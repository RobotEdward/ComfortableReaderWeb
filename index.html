Features

  • Simple, focussed and quick
  • 20 things to read in your pocket, no messing around
  • Full articles with images cached for offline reading
  • Tracks your progress and gets you back to reading super quick
  • Archive, delete, and like articles
  • Animated live tile with background synching
  • Offline changes are synched whenever you connect
  • Smells of rich mahogany
